Did you know that EULAR guidelines recommend maintaining
serum uric acid (sUA) levels below 360 μmol/L (<6 mg/dL)?
In fact, for patients with extensive tophi and a presumed high
crystal load, the therapeutic target may be well below this point to
permit faster tophi reduction. Find out more inside this booth.1-5
